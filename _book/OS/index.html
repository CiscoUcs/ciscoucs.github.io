Operating Systems

Some operating systems are not "supported" by Cisco on UCS, but that doesn't mean they don't run, and in fact, run quite well. Here we will go through some of the installations of common operating systems especially as they relate to DevOps and how to install them quickly. This includes officially supported and unofficially supported OSes.
